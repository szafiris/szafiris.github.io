About me

My Story

I have always been a very creative person with a passion for creating in general, whether it be drawing, videos, websites etc., however I struggled to execute my creativity by hand. My mindset has always better attuned to math and technology rather than drawing and more traditional art forms. I then learned that I could utilize my computer skills to execute my own creative visons. What I lacked in dexterity of the pencil I made up for by learning how to get the technology to do it for me. This led me to my love for coding and animation, in which I can take my creative side and apply it to the code and various technical programs within the Animation Industry

My more visual and creative side has also created a passion for UX/UI as I have a great appreciation for the research and thought required to make an appealing and easy to use website. My time as a UX intern helped to further solidify my interest in the field as I enjoyed the process of research to preliminary design all the way through coding the final product.

I am also a member of the Division 1 Loyola Marymount University Women's Water Polo Team. My time as a student athlete has taught me valuable lessons in time management, discipline, and working well with others under times of great stress.
